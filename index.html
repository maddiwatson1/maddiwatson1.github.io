At a young age, I had found a strong passion for pencil illustrations. In high school I had explored all kinds of mediums for the artwork I created and found myself leaning back towards pencil drawings. Oddly enough, I never thought I'd go to school for graphic design and become a designer.

Come 2014 I will be receiving my graphic design diploma and will be out in the real world. As a student and freelance designer, I have gained a lot of knowledge in all aspects of design and hope to become a logo and layout designer in the future.
